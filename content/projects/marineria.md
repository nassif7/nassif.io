Marineria.it is an Italian maritime recruitment platform. This is their mobile app, built with React Native, Expo, and TypeScript.

The app works without an account. Guests can browse and filter job offers. Crew members can build a profile, apply for positions, and get honest feedback when they don't qualify. Recruiters can post jobs, search candidates by skills or location, and contact applicants directly.

Push notifications go out when a matching job is posted (for crew) or when someone applies (for recruiters). The app is in Italian and English with a toggle in settings.

Login is either OTP or password. Registration is built into the login screen.
